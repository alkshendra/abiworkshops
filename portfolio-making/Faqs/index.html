ABI workshops was started in 2018 by Ar. Ankita Kakani

We saw the acute gap between the coursework of architecture and the professional world and decided to take a step towards bridging this gap by incorporating practical and engaging methodologies in our workshops.

At ABI workshops, we maintain an informal and interactive method of teaching to make the students feel comfortable and involved in the learning process. Our aim is to guide the young aspiring architects to build a strong foundation in their nascent years of learning.
